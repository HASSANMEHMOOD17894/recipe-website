Biryani Receipe

Our Methodology:


INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Yougurt 1/2 KG
Rice 3/2 KG
Onion 1/2 KG
Oil 3/4 KG
Chiken 2 KG

INGREDIENTS

  • Yougurt
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Chiken

RECIEPE

  1. pour oil in the pot
  2. put onion and cook till golden brown
  3. put chiken in the pot
  4. put rice and mix it
  5. now enjoy your Biryani
